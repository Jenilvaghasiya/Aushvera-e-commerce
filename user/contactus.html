Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What makes Ayushvera products different?

Our products combine ancient Ayurvedic wisdom with modern scientific validation. We use only the highest quality, organic ingredients and each batch undergoes rigorous lab testing to ensure purity and potency.

How long does shipping take?

We typically process orders within 1-2 business days. Domestic shipping takes 3-5 business days, while international shipping varies by location (7-14 business days). You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

Are your products suitable for everyone?

Most of our products are suitable for adults of all ages. However, we recommend consulting with a healthcare professional before use if you are pregnant, nursing, have a medical condition, or are taking medications.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk purchasing?

Yes! We offer special pricing for retailers, practitioners, and bulk purchasers. Please contact our wholesale team through the contact form above for more information.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange (minus shipping costs). Products must be unopened and in original condition.
